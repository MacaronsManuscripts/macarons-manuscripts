💖 The maker behind the magic

About Maria

The Maker. The Bookworm. The Dragon’s Human.

Hello, I’m Maria—the heart, hands and slightly chaotic brain behind Macarons Manuscripts. I created this little bookish boutique because I believe books make life better, and beautifully packed happy mail makes it even better.

“Most days, I’m either making something, packing something, reading something—or looking for the thing I put down two minutes ago.”

A little more about me

The Story Behind the Shop

Macarons Manuscripts began with a love of books, creativity and the idea that receiving a parcel should feel like opening a tiny celebration.

📚 Books Are My Love Language

I’m a lifelong reader and a total book addict. Fantasy, romantasy, cosy mysteries, dark romance and magical adventures all have a permanent place on my shelves.

🎁 Happy Mail Matters

I want every Macarons Manuscripts order to feel exciting from the moment it arrives. Every item is chosen, packed and checked with care—usually under Pip’s extremely serious supervision.

✨ Made With Personality

I love creating products that are cosy, funny, magical and useful. The shop is full of magnetic bookmarks, surprise bags, book sleeves, stationery and reader gifts with a little bit of mischief built in.

What matters here

The Heart of Macarons Manuscripts

💌 Thoughtful Details

From packaging to little extras, I want customers to feel that their order was prepared by a real person who genuinely cared about making them smile.

🐉 A Little Dragon Magic

Pip brings warmth, humour and personality to the shop. He helps turn Macarons Manuscripts into more than a place to buy things—it becomes a little storybook world.

📦 Small-Business Care

Every order supports an independent UK business. I pack parcels myself, design new ideas, answer messages and celebrate every lovely review and returning customer.

🌸 Cosy, Cute and Never Boring

I love soft colours, whimsical details and gifts that make readers laugh. The aim is always premium and magical—not bland, generic or overly serious.
