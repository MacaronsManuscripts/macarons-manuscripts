Meet Pip Inkwing

Keeper of the Manuscripts

Every magical bookshop needs a guardian. Ours just happens to be tiny, purple, bespectacled and permanently distracted by books, bookmarks and hot chocolate covered in far too many marshmallows.

“I take my responsibilities very seriously. Apart from the bits involving tidying.”

Pip Inkwing — Book dragon, parcel inspector and suspected sticker thief

Once upon a bookshelf

Pip’s Story

Long before Macarons Manuscripts existed, a tiny dragon made his home between the shelves of a magical library.

While other dragons collected gold, Pip collected stories. He guarded dog-eared paperbacks, rescued forgotten bookmarks and believed every reader deserved a little magic with their next chapter.

One day, Pip met Maria. She was creating a cosy little bookish shop filled with gifts, reading accessories and happy mail. Pip immediately offered to help.

His definition of “help” includes inspecting every parcel, organising magnetic bookmarks, supervising snack breaks and occasionally falling asleep on the packaging.

Together, Maria and Pip created Macarons Manuscripts: a magical bookish boutique for readers who believe there is no such thing as too many books—or too many cute reading accessories.
